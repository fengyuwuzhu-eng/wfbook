# 日志正则表达式
## Linux Syslog
**messages/secure**

`(?<timestamp>\S+ \S+ \S+) (?<hostname>\S+) (?<service>[^\[\]\:]+)(?:\[(?<pid>\S+)\])?: (?<message>.*)` 

**adult**

`type=(?<type>\S+) msg=audit\((?<timestamp>\S+):(?<num>\S+)\): (?<user>[^ ]*) %{DATA:info} msg=\'%{DATA:message}\'$`

**yum.log** 

`(?<timestamp>\S+ \S+ \S+) (?<action>\S+): (?<software>\S+)`
